What Bunky can do for you?

Bunk Calculations

We made calculation very easy. Pre-loaded equations for all your bunking requrements. No Need to copy paste the values, Bunky can automatically take data from University Server. Yaa! Magic!

Analysis

Bunky can analyse your attendanc and advice you what you can do with it. You specify what is your priority before hand and bunky will take that into account while suggesting you what to do.

Plan a holiday

You attended all the classes in the begening! Now time to use your extra attendance. Plan a holiday let you plan bunky for a longer period of time. Bunky will give you clear picture of those classes bunked on your attendance.
